# Stanford AI & Medical Education Initiative Website

## Navigation

Top navbar with 5 tabs: **Home**, **Overview**, **Learn**, **Apply**, **Get Involved** — plus the Stanford initiative branding/logo in the top left.

## Home Page (Full Implementation)

### 1. Hero Section

- Large, visually striking banner with a medical/AI-themed background image
- Prominent display of the **Fundamental Theorem of Informatics**: *"Human + Computer > Human"*
- Tagline reinforcing the initiative's purpose
- CTA buttons (e.g., "Start Learning" and "Get Involved")

### 2. Mission Statement Section

- A concise, compelling mission statement about bridging AI and clinical medicine education at Stanford

### 3. Featured Content Carousel

- Horizontal card carousel (using Embla) showcasing latest features, programs, courses, or tools
- Each card includes an image/icon, title, short description, and a link
- Navigation arrows and dot indicators

### 4. News & Publications Section

- Grid of cards showing recent news articles, research papers, and announcements
- Each card with date, title, summary, and "Read More" link
- Clean, academic styling
